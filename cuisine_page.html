North Indian Cuisine

North india is known for its rich,aromatic and flovourful dishes often cooked with ghee and spices:

  • Biryani: A fragrant rice dish made with aromatic spices and meat or vegetables.
  • Butter Chicken: a creamy,tomato-based curry that's a favourite worldwide.
  • Paneer Tikka: Marinated paneer cubes grilled to perfection.

South Indian Cuisine

South India offers a wide area of dishes with a perfect blend of spices and tangy flovours.

  • Masala Dosa: A crispy rice crepe stuffed with spicy potato filling.
  • Idli and Sambhar: Soft rice cakes served with tangy lenti-based vegetable stew.
  • Chettinad Chicken: A fiery,flovourful chicken dish from Tamilnadu.
